Magnetic fields thread the cosmic web, but they are so faint that no single sightline reveals them. In my 2021 paper I stacked thousands of rotation measures and cross-correlated them against the large-scale structure to place an upper limit on the field strength.

This interactive sandbox lets you toggle the data and watch the detectability floor move: sharper measurements (LoTSS versus NVSS) and larger samples both tighten what we can constrain. It is an early prototype with illustrative values for now, with the real data and a full explainer coming soon.
